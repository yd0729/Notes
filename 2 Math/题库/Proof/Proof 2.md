# Proof 2 可导 $\Rightarrow$ 可微

$$
\begin{array}{l}
\displaystyle
f^{'}(x_0)=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\cfrac{f(x_0+\Delta x)-f(x_0)}{\Delta x}\\\\
\displaystyle
\Rightarrow\cfrac{f(x_0+\Delta x)-f(x_0)}{\Delta x}=f^{'}(x_0)+\alpha\\\\
\Rightarrow\Delta y=f(x_0+\Delta x)-f(x_0)=f^{'}(x_0)\Delta x+o(\Delta x)
\end{array}
$$
